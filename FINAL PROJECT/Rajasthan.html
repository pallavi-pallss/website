RAJASTHAN

Rajasthan, the "Land of Kings," is India's largest state, renowned for its rich cultural heritage and breathtaking natural beauty. The Thar Desert, with its majestic sand dunes, camel safaris, and stunning sunset views, is a must-visit destination. The Aravalli Hills offer scenic valleys, hiking trails, and relaxation, while lakes like Pichola, Fateh Sagar, and Sambhar Salt Lake provide serene boat rides.
